How it all began

'I met my best friend that day' - James

The pair fell in love over a birthday cake in a dusty engineering office in 2013, making awkward conversation. From Essex to Surrey Olivia would travel 60-miles around the M25 to share their love of Monkey Island, literature and giggling for no reason.

Throughout 2014 James and Olivia travelled realising their love of adventure; from the tops of mountains to the hustle and bustle of Capital cities, they wandered with each other by their sides.

'Our relationship just felt so natural from the start' - Olivia

Now residing in a small village in Surrey, James and Olivia spend their lives always looking for the next adventure, from cycle rides in Richmond to spending hours in record shops in Farnham.

Whatever they do, they do it with the love of each other in their hearts.
